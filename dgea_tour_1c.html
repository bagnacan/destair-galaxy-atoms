FastQC and Cutadapt

FastQC is one of the best-liked software for quality control and provides the user with a graphically rendered output. This allows a fast inspection of complex datasets in case of “number of total sequences”, “per base sequence quality”, “per base sequence content”, and “sequence duplication level”. Cutadapt is one of the first released trimming software compared to all mentioned trimming algorithms in the list. Sickle and Trim Galore! toke advantage of Cutadapt, whereby the latter one wraps only Cutadapt and FastQC together.
