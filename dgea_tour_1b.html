Description:

FastQC is one of the preferred tools for quality control. This tool analyses FASTQC data, and produces graphs to allow fast inspection of sequencing data sets.

With its high speed, sensitivity, and customizable settings, PRINSEQ enables users to perform fast filtering and trimming of FASTQ files. This tool also provides a graphical overview of the quality of the provided input data sets. Thus, like FastQC, PRINSEQ can also be used to assess the quality of FASTQ files.
