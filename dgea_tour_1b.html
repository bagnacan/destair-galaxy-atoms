FastQC and Trimmomatic!

FastQC is one of the best-liked software for quality control and provides the user with a graphically rendered output. This allows a fast inspection of complex datasets in case of “number of total sequences”, “per base sequence quality”, “per base sequence content”, and “sequence duplication level”. Trimmomatic offers an easy to use interface, and it is the most used tool for this purpose.
