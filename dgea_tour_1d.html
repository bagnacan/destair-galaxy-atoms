Description:

FastQC is one of the best-liked software for quality control and provides the user with a graphically rendered output. This allows a fast inspection of complex datasets in case of “number of total sequences”, “per base sequence quality”, “per base sequence content”, and “sequence duplication level”. The strength of Prinseq is the speed, sensitivity as well as the highly customizable setting.
