FastQC and Trim Galore!

FastQC is one of the best-liked software for quality control and provides the user with a graphically rendered output. This allows a fast inspection of complex datasets in case of “number of total sequences”, “per base sequence quality”, “per base sequence content”, and “sequence duplication level”. Trim Galore! is a wrapper for Cutadapt and compared to Cutadapt, it offers an easy to use experience.
